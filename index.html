⚡ Power Systems

My research in Power Systems focuses on the operation, planning, monitoring, and control of modern electrical distribution systems. The work includes optimal power flow, voltage regulation, state estimation, renewable energy integration, low-voltage distribution networks, and intelligent control strategies for improving system reliability and efficiency.
